About Property Intelligence NZ

Property Intelligence is New Zealand's independent destination for property financial planning.

Our Mission

In a world of bank-owned calculators and biased advice, we provide Kiwis with the raw, unfiltered data they need to make the biggest financial decisions of their lives. We are not a mortgage broker, and we are not a bank.

The Toolset

Our tools are designed for the modern NZ real estate market, taking into account current interest rate trends and Loan-to-Value Ratio (LVR) requirements. Whether you're a first-home buyer or a seasoned investor, we give you the edge.

Team

Based in New Zealand, we are a group of developers and researchers dedicated to financial transparency. Built for the property market in Morrinsville and across Aotearoa.
